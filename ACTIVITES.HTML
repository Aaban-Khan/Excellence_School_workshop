ACTIVITIES

N.C.C

N.C.C. activities help interested students build discipline, confidence, teamwork, and respect for service. Students learn through guided practice, regular training, and school-level participation that supports their overall development. The activity also helps students understand punctuality, leadership, fitness, responsibility, and the importance of working together with a positive attitude.

SPORTS

Sports are an important part of student life. The school encourages students to take part in outdoor and indoor games so they can stay active, learn teamwork, and develop a healthy competitive spirit. Regular participation in games helps students manage stress, improve focus, follow rules, respect others, and understand that effort and practice are as important as winning.

SCOUTS AND GUIDES

Scouts and Guides activities encourage responsibility, cooperation, leadership, and service. Students take part in guided tasks that help them develop practical life skills and confidence. Through group work, service-based activities, discipline, and problem-solving tasks, students learn to become more aware, helpful, and prepared for real-life situations.

About

Govt. H.S. Excellence School No.1, Dhar supports students through classroom learning, practical activities, discipline, and a respectful school environment. Activities are an important part of this growth because they help students discover interests, build confidence, and learn beyond textbooks.
