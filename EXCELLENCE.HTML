Where learning begins
Govt. H.S. Excellence School No.1, Dhar

Learning with purpose since 1962

Govt. H.S. Excellence School No.1, Dhar is a co-educational government school for Classes 9 to 12 in Dhar, Madhya Pradesh. The school focuses on steady academic growth, discipline, practical learning, and a supportive environment where students can learn with confidence. Our aim is to help students become sincere learners who are prepared for examinations, higher studies, and everyday responsibilities.
The campus includes classrooms, a computer lab, an EFA/A.I. lab, a library, and spaces for sports and school activities. Hindi and English are used for instruction, and the academic session begins in June. Along with classroom teaching, students are encouraged to take part in cultural programs, sports, practical work, and group activities that build confidence and communication.
The school maintains a simple, respectful, and student-friendly environment. Teachers guide students through regular lessons, revision, discipline, and personal attention wherever possible. Students are also supported through textbooks, applicable scholarship opportunities, and hostel support for eligible students who live far from the city.

Media Coverage

Media coverage helps families and the wider community stay connected with school events, student activities, exhibitions, and important announcements. It also gives students a chance to see their learning, teamwork, and participation recognized beyond the classroom. School programs, cultural activities, science work, awareness events, and student participation are often meaningful moments for the community, and clear communication helps those moments reach parents and well-wishers in a simple and positive way.
