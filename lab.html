Laboratories

E.F.A Lab

The E.F.A./A.I. lab supports students in learning digital skills through guided practice, computer-based work, and project activities. It gives students a focused space to explore technology and apply classroom concepts. Students can develop basic computer confidence, logical thinking, safe technology habits, and the ability to use digital tools for learning, communication, and future study.

Security Lab

The Security Lab supports vocational learning through classroom instruction, demonstrations, and guided practice. The space is used to help students understand safety, awareness, and responsible conduct. Lessons focus on discipline, observation, communication, emergency awareness, and the importance of remaining alert and calm in different situations.

Biology Lab

The Biology Lab helps students learn through observation, models, charts, and practical demonstrations. It supports a clearer understanding of living systems, health, and the natural world. Practical learning helps students connect textbook topics with real-world observations, ask better questions, and develop scientific curiosity through careful observation.

Physics Lab

The Physics Lab helps students connect theory with practical observation. Experiments and demonstrations support learning in measurement, motion, electricity, light, and other core science concepts. By seeing principles in action, students improve their problem-solving skills, accuracy, patience, and confidence in understanding scientific ideas.

Chemistry Lab

The Chemistry Lab provides a supervised space for practical learning. Students observe experiments, learn safe lab habits, and understand chemical reactions, materials, and basic scientific methods. Practical chemistry helps students become more careful, organized, and curious while learning how scientific processes are tested and explained.

About

Govt. H.S. Excellence School No.1, Dhar supports students through classroom learning, practical activities, discipline, and a respectful school environment. Laboratory learning gives students a chance to observe, practice, question, and understand concepts more deeply.
